About Me

I am a PhD-qualified ICT engineer, postdoctoral researcher, and academic specialising in wireless communications, heterogeneous network systems, and next-generation (5G/6G) mobile technologies.

My doctoral research at CQUniversity focused on solving real-world challenges in 5G/6G cellular networks, particularly load balancing, interference mitigation, and cell association optimization. Currently, I lead research on agricultural supply chain innovation and network mapping solutions, while supervising emerging engineers and delivering ICT education.
